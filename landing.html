About This Project

OPEN SOURCES of power

A privacy-first public utility project built on PLUG. Location details are community-maintained. We verify information regularly to ensure accuracy and currency.

🎯 Our Mission: Make it easy for vulnerable populations—including unhoused individuals, travelers, and those in digital deserts—to find reliable charging options without sacrificing privacy.

🔐 Privacy Commitment: We collect zero data about your location or device. No sign-in required. No tracking. No history. Pure utility.
